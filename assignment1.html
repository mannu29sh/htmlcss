Soup

Beethoven once said, "only the pure of heaart can make a good soup".Well, here's my attempt to do that!

  1. First heat a pan with oil and add the garlic, ginger. Saute for a minute. Then add the chopped onion and saute until the onion turns translucent.
  2. then add the chopped veggies (potato, carrot, beans) and saute for 2 mins.
  3. Now add the seasoning (turmeric, cumin, coriander, salt, pepper) and mix well. Saute for 1 min.
  4. Add sufficient water (about 3 Cups), give a stir and bring it to a boil. Once the water starts to boil, immediately turn the flame to low-medium, add the tomato and continue to cook until the veggies are fully cooked. Once the veggies are fully cooked, turn off the flame and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  5. Serve hot.

Salad

here's my ingredients and steps for making fresh salad.

List of ingredients

  • one tbsp olive oil
  • half tbsp lemon juice
  • half tbsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps for making salad

  1. Put all your mixed greens and spinach in a large bowl
  2. Add in the cucumbers, carrots, bell pepper and tomatoes
  3. Put in the protein of your choice.
  4. Top with the tasty toppings, which are your pumpkin seeds, basil leaves and chickpeas
  5. In a separate bowl, place all dressing ingredients and whisk until combined
  6. Dress and plate your salad
